Zentra Finance App

Purpose: Create a goal-based savings app that helps users save money quickly for big purchases or events.

Objective: Design a responsive, mobile-first interface that motivates saving through clear goal tracking, personalized tips, and progress visualization.

Role: UX/UI Designer (sole designer on project)

Duration: 4 weeks (from research to high-fidelity prototype)

View the Full Case Study

Research

I began by speaking with potential users to uncover frustrations in saving for big goals. Whether for travel or milestone events, they all wanted two things: clear progress and actionable steps.

Ideation

These insights shaped Zentra's foundation. I created user personas, mapped user flows, and prioritized simple, natural actions like setting a goal, tracking progress, and receiving tips.

Wireframe

The first wireframes reduced friction with fewer screens, clearer labels, and a clean hierarchy. Testing revealed opportunities to make progress more visual and provide feedback at key moments, leading to a progress ring, subtle animations, and a calm, focused color palette.

Design

In high-fidelity design, I emphasized approachability and clarity. Nunito Sans gave headings a warm, modern voice, Open Sans kept body text highly readable, and micro-interactions added life without clutter. Accessibility remained a priority, with color contrast and tap targets meeting WCAG AA standards.
